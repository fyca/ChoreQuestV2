## Data Collection
ChoreQuest collects: names, email addresses (parents only), device information, chore data, reward information, point transactions, activity logs, and optional photos for chore verification.

## Data Usage
Data is used solely for app functionality: managing family accounts, assigning chores, tracking completions, managing rewards, and syncing across devices.

## Data Storage
All data is stored securely in Google Drive through our Google Apps Script backend. Local caching occurs on your device for offline access.

## Third-Party Services
We use Google services (OAuth, Drive API, Apps Script) for authentication and storage. See Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's Privacy
ChoreQuest is COPPA-compliant. Parents create and manage all accounts. Children's accounts require only a name (no email). Parents have full control over children's data.

## Data Sharing
We do not sell or share your data with third parties except as necessary for app functionality (Google services) or as required by law.

## Your Rights
You can access, update, or delete your data through the app. Parents can manage all family accounts. Contact Lostsierraapps@gmail.com for account deletion requests.

## Security
We use encryption, secure authentication (OAuth 2.0), and Google's secure infrastructure to protect your data.
